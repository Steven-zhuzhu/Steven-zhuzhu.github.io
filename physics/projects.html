Quantum Physics Research Projects

Two-Qubit Quantum Entanglement Study (Independent Research 2025)

Explored the mathematical framework of Bell states and simulated entanglement dynamics using Python. Tested CHSH inequality violation with numerical methods, verifying the non-local properties of quantum entanglement.

Bell State \(|\Phi^+\rangle = \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}(|00\rangle + |11\rangle)\)

CHSH Inequality: \(S = |E(a,b) - E(a,b') + E(a',b) + E(a',b')| \leq 2\) (classical) / \(S \leq 2\sqrt{2}\) (quantum)

Double-Slit Experiment Quantum Superposition Analysis

Combined experimental data collection and theoretical derivation to quantify the superposition principle in the double-slit experiment. Analyzed the transition from quantum to classical behavior with varying particle detection methods.

Probability Amplitude: \(\psi(x) = \psi_1(x) + \psi_2(x)\), \(P(x) = |\psi(x)|^2 = |\psi_1|^2 + |\psi_2|^2 + 2\mathrm{Re}(\psi_1^*\psi_2)\)
