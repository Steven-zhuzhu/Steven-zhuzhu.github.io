Grandpaâ€™s Pill: AI-Powered Medication Reminder

My grandfather, now 85 years old, first inspired my interest in science long before I could speak. He now frequently forgets to take his medication. I couldn't accept the possibility that he could be in danger because he missed a simple dose. So I called on several like-minded friends and developed a "smart medication reminder" app.

Key Research Directions

1. AI-Powered Disease Detection

Develop ML models to identify early-stage diseases (e.g., cancer, cardiovascular diseases) from patient data, electronic health records (EHRs), and lab results.

2. AI in Drug Discovery

Use deep learning to predict molecular properties, simulate drug-target interactions, and shorten the drug development cycle from years to months.

3. Medical Imaging Analysis

Build CNN (Convolutional Neural Network) models to analyze X-rays, CT scans, and MRIs, automating lesion detection and segmentation with high precision.

Technical Implementation

  • Programming Languages: Python (NumPy, Pandas, Scikit-learn, TensorFlow/PyTorch)
  • Data Sources: Public medical datasets (TCGA, MIMIC), clinical trial data, medical imaging datasets
  • Model Optimization: Transfer learning, hyperparameter tuning, model interpretability (SHAP/LIME)
