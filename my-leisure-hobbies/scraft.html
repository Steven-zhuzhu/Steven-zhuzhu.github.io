My Stage

The Interrupting Chick

I am passionate about creating and directing stage plays for children.

I work on these plays with a group of friends: I serve as the playwright and director, Yao Jia as the director, Ze Kun in charge of sound effects production, and Xiao Yang responsible for costume design. Our former primary school homeroom teacher has all the students in her current class act as our young performers, and the mothers of these child actors take care of makeup and all backstage logistics.

One of our original children’s plays, The Interrupting Chicken, won the First Prize at the Shenzhen Picture Book Drama Carnival.
