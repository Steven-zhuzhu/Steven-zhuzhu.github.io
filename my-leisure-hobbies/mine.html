The Beauty of Violin - My Musical Prelude

Music is not just sound, but a journey of the soul. For me, the violin is more than an instrument – it is a companion that has walked with me through the years, a bridge between my inner world and the outside, and a language that transcends words.

Since I first picked up the violin in 2017, every note I played has been a footprint of growth. From the clumsy squeaks of the beginner stage to the relatively smooth melodies of today, every step has been filled with sweat, joy, and unspoken emotions.

"The violin is the soul's voice; it speaks what the heart cannot express." – Unknown

My Musical Journey - The Learning Challenge

The journey of learning the violin is never a straight line. It is a process of constant exploration: exploring the balance between bow and string, exploring the resonance between emotion and technique, and exploring the harmony between oneself and the music. This exploration has become an indispensable part of my life.

In those quiet afternoons filled with joy, or evenings clouded by sorrow and stuck with unsolvable problems, the violin was my go-to emotional outlet. It never failed to calm my mind and let me express all my feelings freely without words.

My Musical Journey - Orchestra Experience

Play Video (Download)

A key and precious part of this musical journey was performing on stage with the Shenzhen Poly International Youth String Orchestra. Through consistent daily practice and unremitting polishing of my skills, I worked my way up to become the first violinist of the orchestra.

This experience not only honed my technical skills but also deepened my understanding of music – teaching me the importance of teamwork, timing, and musical expression in a collaborative setting.

My Favorite Violin Piece

My favorite violin piece for appreciation is Zigeunerweisen (Gypsy Airs) by Sarasate — a classic pinnacle among violin pieces. This piece perfectly blends the sorrow and passion of Gypsy culture, with a graceful and melodious melody and full of timbre expressiveness.

Its rich emotional layers make me deeply intoxicated and truly feel the unique artistic charm of the violin. It stands as the pinnacle of virtuosic technique for the violin, embodying the ultimate technical and artistic pursuit of the instrument.

Listen to Zigeunerweisen

Because of its extraordinary artistic value and technical complexity, I have always regarded it as a classic appreciation piece that represents the beauty of violin music, one that constantly inspires my love for violin and pursuit of musical excellence.

Music & Growth - The Hidden Power of Violin

Beyond the notes and melodies, learning the violin has taught me life lessons that extend far beyond the practice room. The patience required to master a single phrase, the resilience to overcome technical plateaus, and the focus to stay present in the music – these qualities have shaped how I approach challenges in all areas of my life.

As an amateur musician, I don't aim for professional mastery, but rather for the joy of connection – connection to the music, to the composers who created it, and to the audience who listens. Every time I play, I am not just performing; I am sharing a piece of my heart.

"Music is the art of thinking with sounds." – Jules Combarieu

Looking back on these eight years, I am grateful for the violin's presence in my life. It has been a teacher, a friend, and a mirror – reflecting my growth, my struggles, and my joys. And as I move forward, it will continue to be a constant reminder that beauty lies in the journey, not just the destination.
