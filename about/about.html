About Wuhua (Steven) Zhu

Being part of Canada's Physics Olympiad National Team, I hold a profound passion for physics and astronomy. As a club leader, I rally peers with similar interests to engage in advanced academic work. I aspire to be the Prometheus who sparks academic inspiration and am committed to contributing to society with my research rigor and hands-on initiatives.

Academic Focus

Competitive Highlights

My journey in physics competitions has been defined by a drive to test my knowledge against global peers—from winning bronze at the 55th IPhO in France (2025) to securing the 2nd place in Canada's CAP Prize Exam (2025). These experiences have deepened my understanding of experimental physics and collaborative problem-solving.

Leadership & Outreach

As the leader of my school's Physics & Quantum Computing Club, I organize workshops, guest lectures, and competition training to make advanced physics accessible to all students. I believe that scientific curiosity thrives in community, and I aim to inspire the next generation of Canadian physicists.
