Club Leader (2024 - Present)

As the leader of the Astronomy Club, I aim to popularize astronomical knowledge and cultivate peers' interest in space science. The club currently has 32 members, covering grades 10-12.

Key Activities Organized:

  • Monthly stargazing sessions (using school telescopes to observe the moon, Jupiter's satellites, etc.)
  • Astronomical knowledge lectures (e.g., "Black Hole Basics" "Mars Exploration Progress")
  • School solar system model exhibition (attracted 100+ visitors)
  • Collaboration with local astronomy association to watch meteor shower events

My Responsibilities:

  • Plan activity schedules and coordinate school resources (telescopes, activity venues)
  • Invite university astronomy students to give lectures
  • Train new members on basic astronomical observation skills
