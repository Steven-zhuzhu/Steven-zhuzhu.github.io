Mission & Purpose

As a volunteer and sound collector for the Cloud Sound Museum, I gather diverse sounds and craft them into audio stories, helping visually impaired children explore the world through hearing.

Sound Collection Scope

My work involves recording sounds from daily lifeâ€”such as urban sounds, the bustling atmosphere of a marketplace, sports grounds, natural environments, and local dialectsâ€”and weaving them into narrated soundscapes. These stories contextualize each audio clip by explaining its time, place, and origin.

Impact & Reflection

From selecting narratives and adjusting pacing to meticulously refining audio details, every step has deepened my understanding that sound is a vital bridge for the blind to perceive the world. By using sound to convey warmth, I hope to enable more visually impaired individuals to experience the beauty and wonder of the world through my voice.

Sound Library

Since joining the team last August, I have been learning to collect various sounds of nature. These audio materials generally require further refinement and organization, and we are currently stepping up the work on this task.
